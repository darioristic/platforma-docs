# Introduction

PLATFORMA is a comprehensive Cloud Control and Billing Platform designed for telecom operators, managed service providers, and enterprises that need to orchestrate cloud infrastructure, manage products, and automate billing at scale.

## What is PLATFORMA?

PLATFORMA provides a unified platform for:

- **Infrastructure Orchestration** — Provision and manage cloud resources across OpenStack, Kubernetes, and hybrid environments
- **Product & Service Catalog** — Define, price, and manage your cloud products and services
- **Order Management** — Automate order lifecycle from request to provisioning
- **Billing & Finance** — Usage-based billing, invoicing, and financial reporting
- **Unified Portals** — Customer-facing and admin portals for complete self-service

## Key Concepts

### Tenants

PLATFORMA is multi-tenant by design. Each tenant represents an organization with isolated data, users, and resources.

### Resources

Resources represent infrastructure components (VMs, containers, networks, storage) that can be provisioned and managed through the platform.

### Products

Products define the services you offer to customers, including pricing rules, configuration options, and provisioning templates.

### Orders

Orders capture customer requests for products and trigger automated provisioning workflows.
