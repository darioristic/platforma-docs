# Billing and Usage

This guide covers invoices, payments, and usage tracking so you can stay on top of your cloud spending.

## Invoices

The Invoices page provides a complete view of your billing history.



### Statistics

Four summary cards appear at the top:

- **Outstanding Balance** -- Total unpaid amount across all invoices (EUR).
- **Total Paid** -- Cumulative payments you have made.
- **Overdue** -- Number of invoices past their due date.
- **Total Invoices** -- Total number of invoices issued to your account.

### Filtering Invoices

Use the tabs to filter the invoice list:

- **All** -- Every invoice regardless of payment status.
- **Unpaid** -- Invoices that still need to be paid.
- **Paid** -- Invoices that have been settled.

### Invoice Table

Each row in the table shows:

| Column             | Description                                                      |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Invoice Number** | A unique identifier (e.g., INV-2026-0042).                       |
| **Status Badge**   | Color-coded: green for Paid, yellow for Unpaid, red for Overdue. |
| **Due Date**       | The date by which payment is expected.                           |
| **Amount**         | Total amount in EUR.                                             |

### Invoice Detail Sheet

Click any invoice to open its detail view.



The detail sheet includes:

- **Timeline** -- Key dates: when the invoice was issued, when it is due, and when it was paid (if applicable).
- **Total Amount** -- The full invoice total with a breakdown of line items.
- **Actions**:
  1. **Download PDF** -- Save a PDF copy of the invoice for your records or accounting.
  2. **Pay Now** -- Available for unpaid invoices. Initiates the payment flow.

### Paying an Invoice

1. Open an unpaid invoice from the Invoices page.
2. Click **Pay Now**.
3. Follow the payment instructions to complete the transaction.
4. The invoice status updates to **Paid** once the payment is confirmed.

## Usage

The Usage page helps you understand your resource consumption and estimate upcoming costs.



### Date Range

Select a time window from the dropdown at the top of the page:

- **Last 7 Days** -- Short-term view for recent activity.
- **Last Month** -- Standard monthly review.
- **Last 90 Days** -- Quarterly overview for trend analysis.

### Usage Metrics

Four metric cards show your consumption for the selected period:

| Metric        | Unit       | Description                                      |
| ------------- | ---------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **CPU Hours** | vCPU-hours | Total compute time consumed across all services. |
| **Memory**    | GB-hours   | RAM usage accumulated over time.                 |
| **Storage**   | GB         | Average persistent storage used.                 |
| **Bandwidth** | GB         | Network data transferred in and out.             |

Each metric card also shows an **estimated cost** in EUR based on your current pricing plan.

### Charts

Two visual charts provide additional insight:

- **Resource Consumption** -- A bar chart comparing CPU, memory, storage, and bandwidth usage across the selected period.
- **Billing Status** -- A pie chart showing the proportion of paid, unpaid, and overdue invoices.

### Usage Summary Table

Below the charts, a summary table lists each service with its individual resource consumption and estimated cost. The final row shows the **Total Estimated Bill** for the selected period.

> **Tip:** Compare the usage summary against your invoices to understand exactly what drives your costs. If you notice unexpected spikes, check the [Services monitoring tab](./my-services.md) for details.
