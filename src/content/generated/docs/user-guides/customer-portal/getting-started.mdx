# Getting Started

Welcome to the Cloud Factory Customer Portal. This guide walks you through creating your account, logging in, and navigating the platform for the first time.

## Creating Your Account

Registration is a quick two-step process.



1. Go to the Customer Portal login page and click **Create Account**.
2. **Step 1 -- Account Details**: Enter your full name, email address, and choose a password (minimum 8 characters).
3. **Step 2 -- Account Type**: Select **Individual** for personal use or **Business** for company accounts. Business accounts require a company name, VAT number, and billing address.
4. Click **Create Account** to finish. You will receive a confirmation email.

### OAuth Sign-Up

Instead of email/password, you can register using a third-party provider:

- **Google** -- Use your Google Workspace or personal Google account.
- **GitHub** -- Ideal for developers who prefer GitHub-based identity.
- **Microsoft** -- Sign in with your Microsoft 365 or Azure AD account.

Click the provider icon on the login page and authorize Cloud Factory to access your basic profile information.

## Logging In



1. Navigate to the Customer Portal login page.
2. Enter your email and password, then click **Sign In**.
3. If you registered via OAuth, click the corresponding provider icon instead.

Authentication uses secure httpOnly cookies. You will remain signed in until your session expires or you explicitly log out.

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you land on the **Dashboard**. It provides a snapshot of your account at a glance.



The top section displays three key metrics:

- **Total Orders** -- The number of orders you have placed.
- **Active Services** -- How many cloud resources are currently running.
- **Outstanding Balance** -- Any unpaid invoice amounts in EUR.

Below the metrics you will find recent activity, quick actions, and links to your most-used services.

## Projects and Workspaces

Cloud Factory organizes resources in a three-level hierarchy:

- **Workspace** -- Your top-level account container. Most customers have one workspace.
- **Projects** -- Logical groupings within a workspace (e.g., "Production", "Staging", "R&D").
- **Services** -- Individual cloud resources deployed inside a project.

This structure lets you separate environments, control access, and track costs per project. You can create and manage projects from [Account Settings](./account-settings.md).

## Navigating the Portal



The header bar is your primary navigation tool:

| Element                | Purpose                                                             |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Workspace Selector** | Switch between workspaces (top-left dropdown).                      |
| **Project Selector**   | Filter the current view to a specific project.                      |
| **Search (Cmd+K)**     | Quickly find services, orders, or settings.                         |
| **Create Menu**        | Deploy a new service -- choose from 6 service types.                |
| **Notifications**      | View alerts for order updates, billing events, and service changes. |

The sidebar links to all major sections: Dashboard, Projects, [Services](./my-services.md), [Orders](./ordering.md), [Invoices](./billing.md), [Support](./support.md), Usage, DNS, and [Settings](./account-settings.md).

## Available Service Types

When you create a new resource through the **Create Menu**, you can choose from:

1. **Enterprise VPS** -- Virtual private servers with dedicated resources.
2. **Private Cloud** -- Isolated cloud environments for your organization.
3. **Managed Kubernetes** -- Container orchestration without the operational overhead.
4. **AI/ML & Data** -- GPU-accelerated instances and data processing platforms.
5. **Managed Databases** -- Fully managed PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, and more.
6. **Object Storage** -- S3-compatible storage for files, backups, and media.

See the [Ordering Guide](./ordering.md) for details on how to deploy a service.

## Theme and Language

- **Theme Toggle** -- Click the sun/moon icon in the header to switch between light and dark mode. Your preference is saved automatically.
- **Language Switcher** -- Select your preferred language from the dropdown in the header. The portal currently supports English and Serbian.
